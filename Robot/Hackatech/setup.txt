## Thymio setup

To setup thymio you neex to first install the <a src="https://wiki.thymio.org/en:thymiosettingwireless">Wireless Thymio Network Configurator</a>

Then follow those instructions:

On the computer:
<ul>
    <li>Plug your dongle into your computer.</li>
    <li>Open the "Wireless Thymio Network Configurator". (supplied with Aseba).</li>
    <li>Choose a channel either 0, 1 or 2 (it must be the same channel for robots in the same network. default channel is 1).</li>
    <li>You can choose a network identifier (default 0x404F).</li>
    <li>Do not change the dongle node identifier (unless you know why).</li>
    <li>Press "Enable pairing"</li>
</ul>
On the Wireless Thymio robot:
<ul>
    <li>Switch on your Wireless Thymio robot (if it was already turned on, turn it off and turn it on again.)</li>
    <li>Put the Wireless Thymio into configuration mode by touching both the right and left buttons for 3 seconds and select the light purple mode by touching several times the right button, then validate by touching the centre button.</li>
    <li>The dongle and Thymio must flash at the same frequency. Bring the Thymio dongle closer until that is the case.</li>
    <li>Exit the Thymio purple mode by turning Thymio off (touch the centre button for several seconds), to save the configuration.</li>
</ul>
Back on the computer:
<ul>
    <li>Press Flash into dongle to save the new settings into it.</li>
</ul>
